The end goal was to increase brand equity among the millennial audience by 7% and grow household penetration by 0.5% points. Interbrand

During my internship at Interbrand NY, I built the 2018 campaign for The Rubin Museum of Art, researching the competitive landscape and identifying key insights, designing creative territories and developing activation concepts around those territories, and delivering a final toolkit with strategic, design, and verbal assets including experience principles and voice implications. I presented the campaign work in a series of four client presentations, leading exercises and discussion in feedback sessions.

The final direction we went with was the Bank of Good Karma. This direction draws from the easily recognizable and understood imagery of dollar bills. It creates an element of surprise when viewers move past their first impression and realize that the campaign content is not actually about money at all but instead focuses on Buddhist teachings and philosophy.
